1. create new PNG-Picture in the Dir: Zombie/Zombie1/assets/textures/blocks
2. go to Dir: Zombie/Zombie1/assets/. Choose the Typ of Block you want to create ("normal" for underground, "onground" for the items on the ground, "topblock" for the second level). Go to the Dir.
3. create new txt-file and consider the parameters for your new Object, given at our Tutotial/blockNormalParameters.txt
4. make the Object appear in your world, let generate it in your world-map! Go to Dir: assets/worldgen/blockpatches. Create a new txt-file, or just copy an old one for your purposes!
